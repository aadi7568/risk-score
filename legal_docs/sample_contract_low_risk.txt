SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between [Company Name] ("Service Provider") and [Client Name] ("Client") on [Date].

The Service Provider agrees to provide consulting services as outlined in the project scope document. The services will be delivered with reasonable care and skill.

2. Payment Terms
Payment is due within 30 days of invoice date. Late payments will incur a standard interest rate of 1.5% per month.

3. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary information shared during the course of this agreement. This obligation remains in effect for 2 years after the agreement ends.

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, the Client will pay for all services rendered up to the termination date.

The Service Provider's liability is limited to the total amount paid by the Client under this agreement. This limitation does not apply to gross negligence or willful misconduct.

The Service Provider warrants that the services will be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.

11. Entire Agreement
This document represents the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements.

12. Amendments
Any amendments to this agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.

13. Assignment
Neither party may assign this agreement without the other party's written consent.

14. Notices
All notices must be in writing and delivered via email or certified mail.

15. Severability
If any provision is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain in effect.
